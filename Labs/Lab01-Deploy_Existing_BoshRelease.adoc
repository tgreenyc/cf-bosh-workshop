==== NOTES: some common things to look for:
- can you bosh ssh into the postgres container?  (hint: there is a *bosh ssh* command, and you probably will need --strict_host_key_checking no option as well)

- from inside the posgres container, can you ps -ef|grep postgres (and see 5 processes?)

- did you change your UUID in postgres.yml?

- did you change your version in postgres.yml?
